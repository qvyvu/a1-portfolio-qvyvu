hi! im vy. i design jewelry pieces for joy and self-expression.

  • my origins

    i started crafting bracelets and keychains as a way to spend more time with myself, and it quickly became a joyful creative practice that i was able to share with others!

  • what inspires me

    there are many inspiring things in the world. i like to look at nature, cute characters from movies or shows i enjoy, etc. a few i have been really into recently are: studio ghibli, the little prince, and orchids

  • materials & process

    i use stainless steel and various beads from online or old/thrifted pieces; each piece is hand-assembled and carefully designed by me

  • ordering & contact

    i accept custom orders via instagram dms — typical turnaround is 1-2 months depending on availability/complexity.
